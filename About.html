PASSION

Having understood the rewards to posterity of empowering women in general, we have chosen to focus on building our African women up in technology as there is an unsettling dearth in this area.

With our goal of putting the continent at the fore-front of innovative solutions, we are concentrating our efforts on women; anchoring on Dr. Aggrey's aphorism that "..educating a woman, educates a Nation"
